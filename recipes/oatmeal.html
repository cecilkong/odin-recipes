Carrot Cake Oatmeal

Description

This Carrot Cake Oatmeal takes all of your favorite flavors of Carrot Cake and serves it in breakfast form along with a serving of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup carrots, finely grated
  • 1/2 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup raisins
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crushed pineapple
  • 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Add water, finely grated carrots, and salt into a medium pot. Turn on stove to high heat and bring to a boil.
  2. Once water is boiling, stir in oats, raisins, cinnamon, allspice, vanilla, walnuts, and crushed pineapple
  3. Reduce heat (~medium-low) and simmer the oats for 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Stir in brown sugar and milk until oats are creamy and consistency desired is reached.
  5. Serve and add desired toppings like more walnuts, coconut shavings, Greek yogurt.
